Our Mission

Built on Four Pillars

Everything we do at Oak Ridge Academy is guided by four core commitments that shape our students into well-rounded, principled leaders.

📚
Academic Rigor
Challenging, standards-aligned curriculum with AP, honors, and enrichment pathways preparing every student for college and beyond.
🌱
Character Development
We nurture integrity, empathy, and resilience — building students who lead with purpose and treat others with genuine respect.
🎭
Arts & Expression
A vibrant arts program — music, theater, visual arts, and digital media — giving every student a voice and a stage.
🏃
Athletic Excellence
24 varsity and JV sports teams teaching teamwork, discipline, and the value of striving for your personal best every day.
